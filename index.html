Meet Joe.

One day, Joe got very sick. He went to the hospital and was admitted to the ER. It turns out to be serious, and he was transferred to intensive care.

A physician orders a few radiology images, and some labs, while Joe is in the ICU. Joe has a couple of procedures done, and is transferred to regular inpatient. Soon after, Joe gets better and is released, with a prescription - and he continues treatment as an outpatient, but at a separate clinic.
 

During his stay, Joe came into contact with at least six different departments: ER, ICU, Regular Inpatient, Radiology, Lab, and Pharmacy. And each department has its own Electronic Medical Records (EMR) system.

Since the data is separate, the clinical picture is not clear. Joe, for example, had an allergic reaction to Lorazepam. This is important information for pharmacists - for this visit or for a visit ten years in the future.

There is unrealized value in coupling data across EMRs.

This is where Health Information Exchange plays a part. CareEvolution's HIE platform enables the secure sharing of clinical data - across multiple care settings in a hospital...

...or, across multiple hospitals and clinics!

On top of this platform, we have written many applications that look across data in multiple care settings. The result is much greater, actionable insight - directly leading to more effective diagnosis and treatment of patients.
